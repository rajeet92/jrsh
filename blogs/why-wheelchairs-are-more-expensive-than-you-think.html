The Technology Behind the Wheelchair

Wheelchairs are not just seats mounted on wheels. They're marvels of engineering, designed to enhance the lives of those with mobility challenges. The technology packed into each wheelchair, especially the electric ones, is cutting-edge. It aims to improve user experience, ensuring smooth navigation, especially in outdoor terrains and tight spaces. Not to forget, these chairs are designed to be lightweight, durable, and comfortable, making them the ideal companion for those who spend extensive hours seated.

The Research and Testing Process

Our team, along with numerous other experts, invests countless hours in research and design. The aim? To cater to different needs and make life better for every user. Once the design is in place, it doesn't directly hit the Wheelchair Market in Delhi. Rigorous testing ensues. We're talking about wheel durability tests, impact tests, and electrical evaluations, to name a few. All these are done to ensure each wheelchair's longevity and safety.
